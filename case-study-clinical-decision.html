Established in 1963, MedCenter Health System has grown to become the region's premier academic medical center, combining cutting-edge patient care with medical education and research. With Level I trauma designation and Magnet nursing recognition, MedCenter serves as a tertiary referral center for complex cases across the Southeast.

As patient complexity increased and physician workloads intensified, MedCenter's leadership recognized the need for advanced clinical decision support tools to maintain their reputation for excellence while ensuring optimal patient outcomes and physician satisfaction.

The Clinical Challenge

Complex medical decisions overwhelming clinical workflows and patient safety

Diagnostic Uncertainty

First-diagnosis accuracy of 72% led to delayed treatments, additional testing, and patient anxiety. Complex cases required extensive consultation time, creating bottlenecks in clinical workflows and impacting patient throughput.

Medical Error Risk

Preventable medical errors occurred in 8.2% of cases, primarily due to information overload, cognitive bias, and inconsistent adherence to evidence-based protocols. Patient safety incidents were trending upward despite quality initiatives.

Clinical Workflow Inefficiency

Physicians spent 65% of their time on documentation and information gathering rather than patient care. Treatment plan development averaged 28 minutes per complex case, contributing to physician burnout and reduced patient satisfaction.

Protocol Adherence Gaps

Only 68% adherence to evidence-based treatment protocols due to knowledge gaps and time constraints. Variation in care quality across departments and shifts created inconsistent patient outcomes and increased liability exposure.

The Minitrix Clinical Solution

AI-powered clinical decision support integrated seamlessly into physician workflows

Minitrix developed a comprehensive clinical decision support system that analyzes patient data, medical history, symptoms, and current evidence-based guidelines to provide real-time diagnostic recommendations and treatment suggestions. The system integrates directly with Epic EHR and provides contextual insights without disrupting clinical workflows.

Real-Time Diagnostic Support

Advanced pattern recognition analyzes symptoms, lab results, and imaging data to suggest differential diagnoses with confidence scores and supporting evidence

Evidence-Based Protocols

Continuously updated treatment recommendations based on latest clinical guidelines, research, and institutional best practices

Safety Alert System

Proactive identification of potential drug interactions, contraindications, and high-risk clinical scenarios with immediate alerts

Workflow Integration

Seamless EHR integration with contextual recommendations appearing at the point of care without workflow disruption
