Founded in 1985, GlobalTech Corp has grown to become a Fortune 500 technology leader, providing enterprise software solutions, cloud infrastructure, and AI services to clients worldwide. With operations spanning 45 countries and a complex web of partnerships, joint ventures, and supplier relationships, GlobalTech processes over 12,000 contracts annually.

As the company expanded globally, their legal department faced mounting pressure to review increasingly complex contracts while maintaining rigorous compliance standards across multiple jurisdictions. Traditional manual review processes were creating bottlenecks that threatened business agility and competitive advantage.

The Legal Challenge

Complex contract volumes overwhelming traditional legal review processes

Manual Review Bottlenecks

Contract review averaged 5.2 days per document, with complex agreements taking up to 3 weeks. The 47-person legal team was overwhelmed by volume, creating delays that impacted deal closure and business operations across all divisions.

Legal Risk Exposure

Inconsistent contract terms and missed risk clauses led to 23% of contracts requiring renegotiation. Hidden liabilities and non-standard terms exposed the company to $8.7M in potential legal and financial risks annually.

Compliance Complexity

Managing compliance across 45 countries with varying regulations created a 34% error rate in regulatory adherence. GDPR, SOX, and industry-specific requirements demanded specialized expertise that strained resources.

Escalating Legal Costs

External legal fees reached $4.2M annually, with 68% spent on routine contract review. Internal legal team overtime costs added another $1.8M, while delayed deal closures cost an estimated $12M in lost revenue opportunities.
